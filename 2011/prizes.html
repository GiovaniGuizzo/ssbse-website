Prizes

Best paper

There will be a prize of €1000 for the best technical track paper at SSBSE 2011, to be decided by the program committee.

The best paper prize is provided courtesy of SSBSE sponsors Berner & Mattner, IBM, Sulake, Softeam and the FITTEST project.

Winner: Shin Yoo, Mark Harman and Shmuel Ur, Highly Scalable Multi Objective Test Suite Minimisation Using Graphics Cards.

Best reviewer

There will be a prize of €500 for the best program committee reviewer at SSBSE 2011, to be decided by the general and program chairs.

The best reviewer prize is provided courtesy of the SWELL Swedish research school in Verification and Validation.

Winner: Marcio Barros.
