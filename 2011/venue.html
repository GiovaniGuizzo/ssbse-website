Venue

SSBSE 2011 will be located with and held following the 8th joint meeting of the European Software Engineering Conference and the ACM SIGSOFT Symposium on the Foundations of Software Engineering - ESEC/FSE 2011 - in Szeged, Hungary.

Szeged is a beautiful University town located in central Europe. It is the third largest city in Hungary, situated close towards the southern border with Serbia and Romania, on both banks of the river Tisza. It has a rich historical past, featuring a range of sensational historical buildings and churches.

SSBSE 2011 will be hosted in the Congress Centre at the University of Szeged (pictured right).
