--- title: "Paper Submission" ---

Paper submission for SSBSE 2009 is now closed. All papers have been reviewed and authors have been notified of the decisions of the program committee. A particular thank you to everyone who participated in the review and rebuttal process.

Final, camera-ready, versions of accepted papers should be prepared by March 13. If you are an author of an accepted paper have not received details by email from Conference Publishing Services of how to prepare and submit the final version of your paper, please contact the program co-chairs.
