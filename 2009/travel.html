--- title: "SSBSE 2009 Travel Information" ---

The venue for the symposium is Cumberland Lodge, Windsor, Berkshire, UK. The Lodge is located in The Great Park, a few miles outside the town of Windsor, and about 20 minutes from Heathrow Airport by taxi. The Lodge is about 10 minutes by taxi from the nearest rail station at Egham; trains from central London take approximately 35 minutes.

Please see the Cumberland Lodge website for detailed information on travelling to the Lodge by road and rail.
