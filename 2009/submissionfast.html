--- title: "Fast Abstract Submission" ---

Fast abstracts should be submitted by 23:59 Samoa time on 20 April 2009 (equivalent to 10:59 UTC/GMT 21 April).

SSBSE 2009 fast abstracts must be submitted electronically via EasyChair. When submitting, please specify 'Fast abstracts' in the 'Paper Category' field.

Contributions must be written in English, adhere to the IEEE style, and be a maximum of 2 pages in length. You can find details about the IEEE style, as well as LaTeX macros and MS Word format, on the IEEE CS Press ftp site.
