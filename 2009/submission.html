--- title: "Paper Submission" ---

Paper submission for SSBSE 2009 is now closed. Thank you for your submissions. We will notify authors by 27 February whether their papers have been accepted as part of the conference program.

SSBSE 2009 regular papers must be submitted electronically via EasyChair here: http://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ssbse2009

Papers must be written in english and must adhere to the IEEE style: maximum 5000 words / 10 pages in length. You can find details about the IEEE style, as well as Latex Macros and MSWord format, on the IEEE CS Press ftp site.
