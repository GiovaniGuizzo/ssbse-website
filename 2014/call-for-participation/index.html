CALL FOR PARTICIPATION
We invite you to participate in the upcoming Sixth International Symposium on Search Based Software Engineering (SSBSE´2014), to be held in Fortaleza, Brazil, with tracks of technical and graduate student papers, SBSE challenge, invited talk and keynotes by renowned researchers. SSBSE is a great venue for undergraduate and graduate students, faculty members, and industrialists to attend. As a delegate at SSBSE´2014, you will learn about the theory and practice of search based methods in a wide variety of areas within the software engineering field.

CONFERENCE VENUE
SSBSE´2014 will take place in the paradisiac Dom Pedro Laguna Beach Villas & Golf Resort, located in the city of Fortaleza, Brazil.

A true paradise between the beach and the golf course… at the finest Luxury Resort in Brazil, in a Hotel without equal.



Occupying a privileged location at the heart of Northeastern Brazil in the state of Ceará, the Dom Pedro Laguna, Beach Villas & Golf Resort, is the first five-star hotel at the luxury Eco-resort “Aquiraz Riviera”. Perfectly integrated into the landscape, Dom Pedro Laguna is quiet and provides total privacy; a paradise between the golf course and the beach, that spreads across a 15.000m2 lagoon with 200m of beach front, the unique and quiet Marambaia Beach.
