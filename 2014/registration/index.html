REGISTRATION FEES
Three types of packages are available to participants (All Inclusive, Conference Only and Accompanying Person).

● All Inclusive Package: this package include all expenses and activities related to your stay during the event and access to all conference activities and materials. It includes:

- 3 Night Accommodation at the luxury conference hotel (Dom Pedro Laguna Beach Villas & Golf Resort), with check-in on august 26th (Tuesday) and check-out on the 29th (Friday). Different rates are available for Single, Double or Triple rooms.
- All meals during the event, that is, dinner on the 26th (Tuesday); breakfast, lunch and dinner on the 27th (Wednesday); breakfast and lunch on the 28th (Thursday) and breakfast and lunch on the 29th (Friday).
- Cocktail Reception on the 26th (Tuesday night).
- Social Event on the 28th (Thursday night).
- Afternoon coffee breaks on the 27th (Wednesday) and 28th (Thursday).
- Airport/Conference Hotel (in case you arrive on the 26th) and Conference Hotel/Airport (in case you leave on the 29th) transfers.
- Access to all conference activities and materials, including the printed proceedings.
