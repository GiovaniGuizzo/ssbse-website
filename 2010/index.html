SSBSE 2010

We are very pleased to announce the 2nd International Symposium on Search Based Software Engineering, SSBSE 2010. The symposium's objective is bring together the rapidly growing international search based software engineering (SBSE) community in a welcoming forum for discussion and dissemination, and to sustain the recent flourishing of interest in the field. We intend to build upon the great success of the inaugural symposium, SSBSE 2009.

The venue for SSBSE 2010 is the beautiful and historic town of Benevento, Italy. Benevento is located in southern Italy, 230 km south-east of Rome, and 80 km north-east of Naples. The closest airports are Naples Capodichino (NAP), Rome Fiumicino (FCO), and Rome Ciampino (CIA), and there are excellent transport links from all three to Benevento. Please see the venue page for more information.

We invite the submission of high quality, original papers on all aspects of SBSE, including theoretical work, research on SBSE applications, empirical studies, and reports from industrial experience. We also invite papers for a special PhD student track that will provide a venue for students to showcase their SBSE research and to receive feedback from senior members of the SBSE research community. Please see the call for papers for submission details and dates.
