--- title: "SSBSE 2010 Call For Fast Abstracts" ---

CALL FOR FAST ABSTRACTS - SSBSE 2010

Fast abstracts feature novel ideas, not yet fully developed or validated. Fast abstract will have a later deadline than technical papers. They should be no longer than 2 pages in CPS proceedings format, and will be reviewed for relevance, soundness, ability to stimulate discussion, and novelty. Accepted fast abstracts will be presented at the symposium and published online on the conference website.

KEY DATES

Fast abstract - notification: 2 August 2010
